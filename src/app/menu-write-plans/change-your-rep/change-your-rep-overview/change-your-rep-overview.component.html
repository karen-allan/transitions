Changing your Representative

If you have previously selected a Primary Representative that you want to change, you must fill in and complete this form. This form must be witnessed by a lawyer or notary public. You can then select your Alternate Representative or if you don't have an Alternate, you can pick a new person to represent you.

Print this form, follow through the directives and store it safely away with your other documents such as your will, your wishes for care, your CPR/DNR forms and your Advanced Care Directive.

If you are not requesting your Primary Representative to be replaced by an Alternate Rep disregard this form.
