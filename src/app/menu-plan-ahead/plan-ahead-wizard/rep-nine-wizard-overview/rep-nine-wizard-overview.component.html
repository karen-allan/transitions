Assign your Substitute Decision Maker with the Rep-9 Form

To qualify as a Substitute Decision Maker, your chosen person(s) must be:
  • 19 or over
  • be mentally capable of making decisions
  • have no dispute with you
  • must not be paid by you for personal care or health care services
  • must not be an employee of a facility in which you reside and receive personal care or health care services from unless this person is your child, spouse or parent
  • have been in contact with you within the year before you need care
To learn more about what a Representative Section-9 advocate can and can not do regarding advocating for your health should you be unable to do so (partially or completely) please go to Choosing your Representative.
