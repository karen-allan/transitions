When do you think that Energy Medicine will take it's place in the world of healing?
We all know that we have blood and bones in our bodies, but unless we are medical professionals most of us don't know how our circulatory, respiratory, digestive or endocrine systems work. We just take it for granted that they do thanks to some smart person in the past who likely went against the established viewpoints and pursued her or his studies until they became accepted.

There have been many pioneers in the field of energy as well and as with anything new their findings have been met with skepticism. It always takes time for new paradigms to find validation and generally it is because of the pressure from patients who are looking beyond accepted, standard medical treatments and have found success with alternative therapies.

Luckily there are dedicated professionals who are gathering evidence based data from which the established medical community can now study.
