Coming back from Victoria I signed up for a 4 month online course with Eden Energy Medicine (now called Eden Method). I was amazed at the wealth of information given to us during this course. I was intrigued, but skeptical and frankly, scared. This was not the me that I had been developing over the last few decades. This was utterly the opposite!

So there I was at loose ends but I was being pulled more and more into this new energy world. Even before the end of the course, I enquired about becoming a practitioner. And much to my delight, I was told that there was room for one more student in the program that was starting the following week. I took the Greyhound bus to Kamloops, B.C. and started my training with a class of 10 students, 2 teachers and an assistant. Having always achieved good marks in the academic world, walking into exams with confidence that I had every fact stored in my brain, I was suddenly terrified. The program challenged me in unique ways, it challenged me to my core. This wasn't just about the ability to memorize facts or follow the logic in code, this was about trusting yourself enough to lay your hands on another human being and connect with them in a new way. I certainly didn't feel like I had any great abilities in this capacity. But I did feel one thing, an overwhelming need to do this. The pressure was intense, with demo exams, written exams and so much information to learn, absorb and understand. But I made it and then crazily enough, signed up for year two!

EDEN - YEAR TWO

Second year was taught in Phoenix, Arizona to approximately 100 students from around the world. Although most students were from the US, many practitioners flew in from Australia, Brazil, England, New Zealand, Israel and like me - Canada.

The students were mostly women but women of all ages, from 20 somethings to 70 somethings. I was told that one of the instructors was a dynamo in her 80s, still teaching and lecturing. There were nurses, physiotherapists, doctors, principals, police detectives, engineers, ex-cops, business owners, acupuncturists, midwifes, massage therapists, Chinese Medicine doctors, naturopaths, Broadway dancers, accountants, artists, Reiki Masters, ex-marines, professional singers, psychologists, teachers and more. Some had overcome terrible diseases like Cancer, some were still fighting them. Each and every student had incredible stories of life challenges and victories. I listened as they talked about death, divorce, suicide, the pains of having babies, the pains of having teenagers and the pain of having adult children:). One had been raised by Holy Bible thumping Evangalists, one was blind, getting help from a friend, one had PTSD from his time in Iraq. The bravest I think, were the physicians, nurses and physician assistants - going against mainstream medicine.

EDEN - YEAR THREE

Year 3 was an post-certification advanced year taught in San Diego, California with about 50 of us. The first fall class spent in a god awful Holiday Inn. The next winter class I shared an Airbnb with some lovely Americans (this was a big deal for me as I always wanted my own space to recuperate after class). But my Airbnb roomies were an unbeleivably wonderful crew of friends. I met so many other interesting people I now call friends.

I decided I needed to attend Year 3 as we were required to perform 100 sessions on as many different clients as we could find. I felt like I had all this theory floating around in my head, but needed to put it to practice. I needed, once again, to step out of the safety of academia and into the world of hands on sessions. I heard stories of bravery, tragedy, loss and survival and was brought to tears many times just looking around the big conference room at all the loving souls. Donna's daughter, Tanya would lead each day with the Daily Energy Routine, Tanya version. It involved music, movement and fun. But it also involved being okay with looking silly.

ABOUT ENERGY MEDICINE

I know that many people think that energy medicine is just a bunch of nonsensical woo woo, a new age practice that takes no critical thought or intelligence to master. However I found out very quickly that the level of study and commitment was just as intense as was my computer science education. And although the level of knowledge was vast and and challenging, it was nothing compared to the level of emotional and personal growth it demanded. Even after 3 years of study, however, I am still searching for more answers, researching new concepts and expanding my knowledge about energy. With that, it is my hope that I can help not only put into words some of the basic concepts I have learned but I hope to put them together in an easy to understand format for you.

I believe that this information should be shared, not provided only after you send me your email address, or sign up for this free course or this free event that starts a torrent of spam to your email account. I want to share, partly for my own sake. We learned so much, so many different protocols and theories that you have to stay engaged to retain it all. When I write about it, I research and learn more so it helps me and hopefully you too.

That being said, there are so many protocols that we were taught that can only be performed by qualified practitioners. There is a new trend that is all about telling novices that with just a few courses they can 'heal themselves'. I find that to be tremendously unhelpful and duplicitous and sets them up for disappointment. I, as a practitioner would never say I could heal anyone. I can only use my knowledge and skills to get them on the table and 'follow the energy'. And if any healing does take place, it is because of the commitment and faith that the client has within to do the exercises, the homework and take care of themselves in all areas of their life. So my emphasis here is not on reeling in a ton of new clients although that would be great if you want to book a session. It is simply about sharing knowledge and helping in my small way, to provide enough tools for my readers to try it out for themselves.

Overcoming doubt, fear, cynicism - developing trust and faith - that energy is real and is affected by everything we do, think and feel - well that took a lot of years and a lot of faith. Faith in the work, faith in myself and faith that people in need will find me.
