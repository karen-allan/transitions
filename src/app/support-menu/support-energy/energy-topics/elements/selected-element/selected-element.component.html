    Exercises for {{selectedElement.name}} Element

    Combining sound and movement can assist {{selectedElement.name}} element to rebalance. Add these exercises to your Daily Energy Routine and note any changes in your mood or energy levels.
    WATER: Blowing out the Candle (by Donna Eden - Energy Medicine)
    Sit on the floor and bend your knees so you can circle your arms around them or just cross your arms and place your fingers underneath your armpits. While you look ahead and up at an imaginary or real candle, picture of look at the blue flame. As you do, rock gently back and forth while inhaling deeply and blowing out with an audible WOOOOHH sound. You can also picture all of your fears, worries, concerns, guilty feelings, everything inside this flame. On blowing out the candle you are burning up these negative thoughts and feelings. This will help dispel fear.
    WATER: Releasing Toxins (by Donna Eden - Energy Medicine)
    Again sit on the floor with the soles of your feet touching each other and hold your toes around the outsides of your feet. Holding on, lean back so you are arching your back and stretching your neck. Inhale and then on the exhale bend forward as far as you can.
    Expelling the Venom (By Donna Eden - Eden Energy Medicine)
    Stand with your feet apart. Inhale and lift your arms straight up over your head and make a fist with each hand. Swing your arms down in front of you and as you do, open up your hands and forcefully say SHHHHHHHHH as you swing your arms down to the floor. Repeat again a few times. On the fourth swing move with intensity and intent, letting all of your anger out as you tell the world - SSSSHHHHHHHHH!!!!! As you rise back up, draw your hands up the middle of your trunk to your lips as you inhale. Reach them up to the sky and on the exhale, lay them on your heart.
    Taking down the Flame (BY Donna Eden – Eden Energy Medicine)
    Stand with your hands on your thighs. Inhale as you circle your arms up over your head. Touch your thumbs together as they point down. Exhale, inhale and exhale again all while making a HHAAAAAAA sound. As you exhale slowly move your thumbs down to the top of your head, at the Crown Chakra. Inhale again and on the exhale move your thumbs to your Third Eye Chakra, just between your eyebrows at the center of your forehead. Exhale and HAAAAAAA. Inhale and move to your Heart Chakra and repeat. I like to continue down all chakras, the Solar Plexus (from the bottom ribs to the navel) the Sacral (from the navel to the pubis) and the Root Chakra (from pubis to top of thighs). Once there flatten your hands and smooth them down your inner thighs all while saying HAAA. Trace to the feet and then trace back up along the inner legs and the very center of your trunk. Land on your heart and exhale.
    Cradling the Baby (by Donna Eden – Energy Medicine)
    While standing put your hands over your belly. Pretend your belly is a tender infant that you are cradling. Show it the love. As you cradle yourself, breathe in through the back of your throat and on the out breath make an EHHHHH sound with your throat. Don’t worry about sounding like Darth Vador! Raise your arms up over your head and reach for the sky, one arm and then the other twice on both sides while holding your breath. On the exhale bend over and touch the insides of each foot. Give them a good stretch so your bottom is pointing upwards. Breathing in again trace your hands up the insides of your legs and up the center of your trunk. At the pelvis swing out to your sides and trace up your sides and rub the area at the edges of your rib cage sides.
    Humans Touching the Divine (by Donna Eden – Energy Medicine)
    Stand with your hands on your outer thighs. Exhale, inhale and as you do so raise your arms to your sides and looking up, reach up to the heavens. Think of reaching out to your loved ones as well as the whole world. As you exhale make the SSSSSSSSS sound and draw your arms inward and round yourself, not quite touching, as you ‘hug the world’. Release your sorrow and grief with each out breath. As you inhale, again stretch out your arms as you invite the world into them while making the SSSSS sound again. Exhale and again, fold yourself under to accept the world and all of it’s grief. When you feel ready touch your fingers together on the exhale movement and then trace them up your chest to your Heart Chakra.
