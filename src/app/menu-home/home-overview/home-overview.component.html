How do we live better?

Not that long ago it was very common for folks to go to their local healer whenever they or a family member were ill or in some kind of distress. If they were having a baby the midwife and/or the women of the village helped the mother give birth. If a person was sick they often had a wise elder, a herbalist or a doctor who did house calls. And in death, the family was there by their side, helping them transition.

How do we get back to taking responsibility for our own health? We have separated our bodies into parts and we have specialists who work on these parts and they send us for test after test. They may confer with one another but the doctor who is supposed to provide the big picture, the general practitioner, is often not equipped to put it all together - because it has been very effectively taken apart!
